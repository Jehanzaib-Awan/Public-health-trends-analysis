# Public Health Trends Analysis: Project Summary
## Overview
This project leverages World Bank-style longitudinal data to evaluate the relationship between healthcare investment (as a % of GDP) and population health outcomes.

## Key Insights
1. **Correlation:** There is a positive correlation between health expenditure and life expectancy in developed nations.
2. **Growth Trends:** Developing nations show the most rapid improvements in infant mortality rates over the 2015-2024 period.
3. **Regional Disparities:** Significant gaps persist between high-income and low-income countries in life expectancy metrics.
